Il suono del tempo

Il suono del tempo (The sound of time) is a musical instrument that collects on the internet the historical meteorological information of the city of London, to be used as input for a data-driven sequencer that orchestrates visuals and sound. The instrument investigates the possibilities of the use of databases as musical notation, employing the atmospheric variations as criteria for compositional decisions.

The project is interdisciplinary as it intersects the fields of music, visual arts, and computing. It also relies on several fields of studies and programming techniques such as digital sound processing and synthesis, API management, logic, and Object-Oriented Programming.

It consists of a sound art installation composed of speakers and an object equipped with a display that ironically simulates the household weather forecast station, showing the data variations as texts, colours, graphics, and particles.
