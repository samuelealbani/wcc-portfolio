Samuele Albani

Samuele Albani was born in San Marino in 1982. At the age of 5 he began to study music and developed his musical training at the Bruno Maderna Conservatory of Cesena and the Music Academy 2000 in Bologna. In 2015 he enrolled in the School of New Technologies of the Arts at the Academy of Fine Arts of Brera in Milan. Theatrical performances: he created the projections of Zaaz who wins the R-Evolution competition and is staged at the National Theater of Milan, 2017. Main exhibitions: “Late at Tate” at the Tate Britain Museum in London, 2014; “Accademia Aperta” at the Academy of Fine Arts of Brera in Milan, 2016 and 2017; “E la luce fu” at Galleria San Fedele in Milan, 2017, “Trame” at Spazio City Art in Milan, “Tra Luce e Tenebre” at Galleria San Fedele in Milan, 2017. Selected at the workshops and shows: Studio Azzurro Creative Stations at Spazio Mil in Milan, 2016; “thec lassroom # 5” by Seb Patane at the Piper Hall of Artissima 2017 in Turin. Wins the Premio San Fedele Visual Arts 2017 with “Catarsi”. From 2018 collaborates with Studio Azzurro as media-artist and creative coder.
