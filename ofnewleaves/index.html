ofNewLeaves()

ofNewLeaves() is an installation relying on an autonomous generative system of drawing and animation of computational botanical shapes.

As algorithmic seeds, little boxes of New Leaves are disseminated on the soil suggesting always new archetypical forms of life through the drawings of growing curves.

ofNewLeaves() is also a technical study representing the first step of the design of a system of drawing and animation ideated by experimenting the aesthetic possibilities of a combination of programming techniques and C++/openFrameworks features, such as Object-Oriented Programming, trigonometry, C++ data structures and Catmull-Rom splines (Catmull and Rom, 1974).
